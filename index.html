Our story

Rooted in compassion,
driven by impact.

Founded in 2018 as a grassroots response to limited healthcare access, Ramba Health Initiative was formally chartered under the Official Seal in 2026 — a milestone marking a decade of growth and recognition. Today, we partner with local clinics, volunteer networks, and global health experts.

We believe health is a right, not a privilege. Through culturally sensitive programs, we've reached over 24,000 individuals with life-changing interventions — from maternal care to mental health awareness. Our official seal represents integrity, excellence, and an unwavering promise of service.

What we deliver

Flagship programs & initiatives

Each program addresses urgent health gaps with dignity and evidence-based methods, bearing the Ramba Health Official Seal.

Mama & Child First

Prenatal support, skilled birth attendance, postnatal care, and vaccination drives — reducing maternal mortality through education and direct aid.

Respiratory Wellness

Screenings for tuberculosis, asthma management, and clean-air awareness. Partnering with local pharmacies to ensure affordable medication.

Mind & Soul Circles

Community-based mental health first aid, trauma counseling, and peer support groups. Breaking stigma, one conversation at a time.

WASH Program

Building wells, distributing hygiene kits, and educating families on infection prevention — the foundation of lasting community health.

NCD Prevention

Non-communicable disease screening (hypertension, diabetes) and lifestyle coaching. Regular health camps in underserved areas.

Youth Health Ambassadors

Empowering young leaders to advocate for sexual health, substance-abuse prevention, and community peer education.
