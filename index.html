Our Focus

Three Focus Pillars

Addressing Rwanda's most pressing health challenges through integrated, community-centred approaches.

01 — MCH

Maternal & Child Health (MCH)

Reducing preventable maternal and child mortality through integrated surveillance, community health workers, and early detection systems.

02 — NCDs

Non-Communicable Diseases (NCDs)
