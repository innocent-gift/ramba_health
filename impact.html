Results & Accountability

Impact

RHI tracks 8 Key Performance Indicators across our programme domains to drive accountability, learning, and continuous improvement.

Our Indicators

8 Key Performance
Indicators

RHI's 8 Key Performance Indicators are tracked across all programme domains to ensure transparent accountability to communities, donors, and government partners.

Request Impact Report →

RHI is committed to rigorous, transparent measurement. Our KPI framework aligns with Rwanda's HMIS reporting standards and international health sector accountability frameworks.

Data is collected quarterly, validated by independent reviewers, and published in our annual programme report.

📊
Programme Reach
Total beneficiaries reached across all RHI programme areas by district and sector.
👥
Workforce Capacity
Number of CHWs and health workers trained, certified, and actively deployed.
🏥
Health Outcomes
Measurable improvements in maternal, child, and NCD health outcome indicators.
🔄
Behaviour Change
Uptake of evidence-based health behaviours among programme participants.
📱
Digital Engagement
Active users of RHI digital health platforms and mHealth tools by programme.
💰
Financial Sustainability
Revenue diversification ratio and cost-per-beneficiary across programme areas.
🔬
Research Output
Publications, policy briefs, and evidence contributions to Rwanda's health knowledge base.
🤝
Partner Engagement
Active partnerships with government, donors, and community organisations.
