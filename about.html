Who We Are

About RHI

Rwanda's integrated Blended Value organization advancing evidence-based, digitally enhanced, community-centred health solutions across Maternal & Child Health, NCDs, and Global Health Security.

The Challenge

Problem Statement

Rwandan Community Health Workers, mamo families and families face significant barriers to accessing integrated health solutions. Fragmented services, limited digital infrastructure, and gaps in community-based delivery create a persistent health equity challenge.

RHI bridges this gap through digitally-enhanced, community-centered health delivery that connects the first and last mile of Rwanda's health system.

See Our Approach →

Our Mission

To advance Rwanda's health outcomes through integrated, evidence-based, digitally enhanced, and community-centred blended value health solutions.

Our Vision

A Rwanda where every community has access to equitable, high-quality, and sustainable health services.

Established

Founded in 2026 as Rwanda's first dedicated Blended Value Health Organization.

Why Integration

Integration Rationale

Sustainable health impact requires convergence — across service domains, digital tools, and community trust.

01

Evidence-Based Design

All RHI programmes are grounded in rigorous evidence, local data, and continuous learning cycles that ensure interventions are effective and replicable.

02

Digital Enhancement

We embed digital tools — from mHealth platforms to data dashboards — to increase reach, efficiency, and the quality of community health delivery.

03

Community Centred

Programmes are co-designed with communities, deploying trusted Community Health Workers as the primary interface for health service delivery.

04

Blended Value

Our model ensures that health interventions simultaneously generate social impact and financial sustainability, enabling long-term programme viability.
